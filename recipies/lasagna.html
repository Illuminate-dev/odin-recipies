Chicken Taco Lasagna

Description

This Southwestern Chicken Taco Lasagna is guaranteed to take family taco night to a whole new level. Tostadas form the "lasagna" noodles and the gooey layers are filled with everything we all love about Southwestern cooking. Rotisserie chicken, taco sauce, a dreamy cream cheese layer. black beans, corn and of course, lots of gooey cheese.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4 inch strips
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 2 (1 ounce) packets taco seasoning mix
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup salsa
  • salt and ground pepper to taste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package no-boil lasagna noodles, or to taste

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, lime juice, and taco seasoning in a large bowl. Stir to make sure chicken is evenly covered.
  2. Cook chicken mixture over medium heat until firm about 15 minutes.
  3. Mix 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, sour cream, and salsa in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Put a few spoonfuls of the sour cream mixture in the bottom of a 9x9-ich baking dish. Layer noodles and chicken on top. Cover with remaining 1 cup mozzarella.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven until noodles are tender and cheese is melted, about 1 hour.
